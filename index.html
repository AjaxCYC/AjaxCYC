Our Initiatives.

The Ajax Youth Council is divided into 3 teams: Transportation, Finance, and Civic Engagement. Each team has their own set of goals which are outlined below.

Transportation.

This team aims to improve issues expressed by the youth of Ajax about their transit options. This include high fares, schedules, safety and an overall experience while taking transit. We want to make it easier for others to get around the Durham Region. We hope to collaborate and build a safer transportation option for youth in Ajax.

Finance.

This team hopes to help educate Ajax's youth on matters related to financial literacy and employment through events and infographics. From conversations about credit to cryptocurrency, the financial literacy team will be holding valuable workshops on a monthly basis for youth to join.

Civic Engagement.

This team is responsible for running a social media campaign to address the lack of civic engagement in Ajax, specifically among youth. This campaign aims to educate and engage youth about the political system, and hence become more active in their community. They are also planning various events to allow youth to participate in the political process.

Who We Are.

Prime Minister Justin Trudeau has said that “young voices must be part of our decision making process” As a part of the Government’s commitment to meaningful youth engagement, the Ajax CYC was founded to bring youth together from Ajax to engage with the federal political process.
